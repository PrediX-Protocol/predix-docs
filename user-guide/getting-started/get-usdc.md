# Get Test USDC

PrediX uses **USDC** (a stablecoin pegged to $1 USD) for all trading. On testnet, you can get free test USDC.

## Faucet — Get $10,000 Test USDC

### Step 1: Make Sure You're Connected

Your wallet should be connected (see [Connect Wallet](connect-wallet.md)).

### Step 2: Click "Faucet" or "Get USDC"

On the PrediX app, find the **Faucet** button. Click it.

### Step 3: Confirm Transaction

Your wallet will ask you to confirm a transaction. Click **"Confirm"**.

> 💡 This costs a tiny gas fee (less than $0.01 in ETH).

### Step 4: USDC Arrives

**$10,000 test USDC** will appear in your balance within seconds. You're ready to trade!

## Check Your Balance

Your USDC balance is shown at the top of the trading panel:

```
Balance: $10,000.00
```

## Important Notes

> ⚠️ **Test USDC has no real value.** This is testnet — for learning and testing only.

> ⚠️ You can request USDC from the faucet multiple times if needed.
