# Connect Your Wallet

## What You Need

- A Web3 wallet (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, Rainbow, etc.)
- A small amount of ETH on Unichain for gas fees

## Step by Step

### Step 1: Open PrediX

Go to the PrediX trading app in your browser.

### Step 2: Click "Connect Wallet"

You'll see a **"Connect Wallet"** button in the top right corner. Click it.

### Step 3: Choose Your Wallet

Select your wallet from the list:
- **MetaMask** — Most popular, browser extension
- **Coinbase Wallet** — Easy for beginners
- **WalletConnect** — For mobile wallets
- **Rainbow** — Mobile-first wallet

### Step 4: Approve Connection

Your wallet will popup asking to connect. Click **"Connect"** or **"Approve"**.

### Step 5: Switch to Unichain

If prompted, switch to the **Unichain Sepolia** network:

```
Network Name: Unichain Sepolia
Chain ID: 1301
RPC URL: https://sepolia.unichain.org
Currency: ETH
```

> 💡 Most wallets will add the network automatically when you connect.

### Step 6: Done!

You'll see your wallet address in the top right. You're connected!

## Need ETH for Gas?

Transactions on Unichain cost a tiny amount of ETH (less than $0.01 per trade). Get testnet ETH from the Unichain faucet.
