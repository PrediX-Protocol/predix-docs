# Cancel Orders

## Cancel a Limit Order

If you placed a limit order that hasn't filled yet:

1. Find your open order in the **Orders** section
2. Click the **"Cancel"** button next to the order
3. Confirm in your wallet
4. Your locked funds are returned immediately

```
Before cancel: $30 USDC locked in BUY order
After cancel:  $30 USDC back in your wallet
```

## What Gets Returned?

| Order Type | What's returned |
|-----------|----------------|
| BUY YES/NO | USDC (your deposit) |
| SELL YES | YES tokens |
| SELL NO | NO tokens |

## Can I Cancel After Market Expires?

**Yes!** You can always cancel your orders, even after the market expires. Your funds are never locked forever.

> 💡 If a market expires with your orders still open, anyone can cancel them for you — but the refund always goes to **you** (the order owner).

## Partially Filled Orders

If your order was partially filled (e.g., 40 out of 100 shares filled):

```
Original: BUY 100 YES at $0.35 → locked $35
Filled: 40 shares
Cancel remaining: 60 shares → refund $21 USDC
You keep: 40 YES shares
```
