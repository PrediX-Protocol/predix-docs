# YES and NO Shares Explained

## What Are They?

When you trade on PrediX, you're buying **tokens** (digital shares):

- **YES token** = "I think this will happen"
- **NO token** = "I think this won't happen"

## How Much Are They Worth?

**Before market ends** (while trading):
- YES and NO have market prices between $0.01 and $0.99
- Prices change based on what traders buy/sell

**After market ends** (resolution):
- The **winning** token = **$1.00** (you can redeem for USDC)
- The **losing** token = **$0.00** (worthless)

## Example

```
Market: "Will Chelsea beat Man City?"

Before match:
  YES (Chelsea wins) = $0.35
  NO (Chelsea doesn't win) = $0.65

After match — Chelsea wins 2-1:
  YES = $1.00 ← Winners!
  NO = $0.00

After match — Man City wins 3-0:
  YES = $0.00
  NO = $1.00 ← Winners!
```

## What Can You Do With Shares?

| Action | How | When |
|--------|-----|------|
| **Buy** | Pay USDC → get YES or NO | Anytime before market ends |
| **Sell** | Sell YES or NO → get USDC | Anytime before market ends |
| **Hold** | Wait for resolution | Until market resolves |
| **Redeem** | Winning shares → $1 USDC each | After resolution |

## How Many Should I Buy?

Your shares = your potential payout.

```
Buy 50 YES at $0.40 → Cost: $20
If YES wins → Payout: $50 → Profit: $30
If NO wins → Payout: $0 → Loss: $20
```

> 💡 **Tip**: Only invest what you can afford to lose. Start small ($25-$50) while learning.

## Multi-Outcome Markets

Some markets have more than 2 outcomes:

```
"Who will win the 2028 US Election?"
  → Trump YES/NO tokens
  → DeSantis YES/NO tokens
  → Newsom YES/NO tokens
  → Harris YES/NO tokens
  → Other YES/NO tokens

Only ONE outcome can win. Buy YES on who you think wins.
```
