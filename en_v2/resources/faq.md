## Trading

**Can I lose more than I invested?**
No. Your maximum loss is the amount you paid for tokens. YES/NO tokens cannot have negative value. If you buy 100 YES at $0.60, your maximum loss is $60 (if the market resolves NO).

## Technical

**How do I get test USDC?**
Use the [Unichain Sepolia Faucet](https://faucet.unichain.org) or mint directly from the test USDC contract. See [Quick Start](../getting-started/quick-start.md).

**Is PrediX audited?**
Yes — 264 tests, 3 independent AI audits, Slither analysis, fuzz testing. See [Security](../contracts/security.md).

**What happens if the oracle fails?**
Emergency resolution activates 7 days after market end time. If that also fails, refund mode returns USDC proportionally. See [Resolution](../concepts/resolution.md).
