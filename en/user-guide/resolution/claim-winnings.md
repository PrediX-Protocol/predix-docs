# Claim Winnings (Redeem)

## When a Market Resolves

After the market ends and the oracle determines the outcome:

```
Market: "Will BTC hit $85K?" → Result: YES ✅

Your YES shares → worth $1.00 each
Your NO shares → worth $0.00
```

## How to Redeem

1. Go to the resolved market
2. You'll see a **"Redeem"** button
3. Click **"Redeem"**
4. Confirm in wallet
5. USDC sent to your wallet instantly

```
You had: 65 YES shares
Outcome: YES wins
Redeem: 65 × $1.00 = $65 USDC → your wallet
```

## What If I Lost?

If you hold **losing** shares:

```
You had: 50 YES shares
Outcome: NO wins
Your YES shares = $0.00
Nothing to redeem. No action needed.
```

> You don't lose anything extra — just the amount you originally paid for the shares.

## What If I Hold Both YES and NO?

```
You had: 30 YES + 20 NO
Outcome: YES wins

Redeem YES: 30 × $1.00 = $30 USDC ✅
NO shares: worthless ($0) ❌
Total received: $30
```

## Can I Forget to Redeem?

There's **no deadline** for redemption. You can redeem anytime after resolution. Your winnings don't expire.

## Multi-Outcome Markets

For markets like "Who wins the election?":

```
You hold:
  Trump YES: 50 shares
  Harris YES: 30 shares

Winner: Trump (index 0)

Redeem:
  Trump YES: 50 × $1.00 = $50 ✅
  Harris YES: worthless ❌

  Also: Harris NO shares are winners!
  (because Harris didn't win)
```
