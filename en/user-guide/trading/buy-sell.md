# Buy & Sell Shares

## Buying Shares (Market Order)

A market order buys immediately at the best available price.

### Buy YES
1. Select a market → Click **YES**
2. Choose **"Market"** order type
3. Enter USDC amount (e.g., $50)
4. Review estimate: "~130 YES shares"
5. Click **"Buy YES — $0.38"**
6. Confirm in wallet → Done!

### Buy NO
Same process but click **NO** instead:
1. Click **NO**
2. Enter amount
3. Click **"Buy NO — $0.62"**

## Selling Shares

### Sell YES
1. Switch to **"Sell"** tab
2. Click **YES**
3. Enter number of shares (or click **MAX**)
4. Review estimate: "~$48 USDC"
5. Click **"Sell YES"**

### Sell NO
Same but select **NO**.

## Limit Orders

Want a specific price? Use a **limit order**.

### Place a Limit Buy
1. Switch to **"Limit"** order type
2. Set your price (e.g., $0.30)
3. Set amount (e.g., 100 shares)
4. Click **"Place Order"**
5. Your order waits until someone sells at $0.30

```
Cost: 100 × $0.30 = $30 USDC (locked until filled or cancelled)
```

### Place a Limit Sell
1. **"Sell"** tab → **"Limit"**
2. Set your price (e.g., $0.70)
3. Set amount
4. Click **"Place Order"**

### Cancel a Limit Order
- Find your open orders in the order list
- Click **"Cancel"**
- Your locked USDC or tokens are returned immediately

## Price Rules for Limit Orders

```
Minimum price: $0.01
Maximum price: $0.99
Step size: $0.01 (you can set $0.35 but not $0.355)
Minimum order: $1.00
Maximum open orders: 50 per market
```

## Market Order vs Limit Order

| | Market Order | Limit Order |
|---|---|---|
| Speed | Instant | Waits for your price |
| Price | Best available now | Your exact price |
| Good for | Quick entry/exit | Getting a better deal |
| Risk | May get worse price (slippage) | May never fill |

> 💡 **Tip**: Use market orders for speed, limit orders for price control.
