# Your First Trade

Let's buy some YES shares! This takes about 2 minutes.

## Step 1: Choose a Market

Browse the markets list. Pick one that interests you. For example:

```
"Will BTC be above $85,000 by 4PM tomorrow?"
YES: $0.38    NO: $0.62
```

Click on the market to open the trading panel.

## Step 2: Select YES or NO

- Click **YES** if you think it will happen
- Click **NO** if you think it won't

For this example, let's buy **YES**.

## Step 3: Enter Amount

Type how much USDC you want to spend. Start small:

```
Amount: $25
```

You'll see the estimate:

```
Est. received: ~65 YES shares
Price per share: ~$0.38
```

## Step 4: Click "Buy YES"

Click the green **"Buy YES — $0.38"** button.

### First Time? Approve USDC

If this is your first trade, you'll need to **approve** USDC first:

1. Click **"Approve USDC"**
2. Confirm in your wallet
3. Wait a few seconds
4. Now click **"Buy YES"**

> 💡 You only need to approve once. Future trades skip this step.

## Step 5: Confirm in Wallet

Your wallet will popup. Check the details and click **"Confirm"**.

## Step 6: Done! 🎉

You now own YES shares! Check your position:

```
Your Position:
  YES  65 shares
```

## What Happens Next?

| If YES wins | If NO wins |
|------------|-----------|
| Your 65 shares → $65 USDC | Your shares → $0 |
| Profit: $65 - $25 = **$40** | Loss: **$25** |

**You can also sell anytime before the market ends** to take profit or cut losses.

## Want to Sell?

1. Switch to **"Sell"** tab
2. Enter how many shares to sell
3. Click **"Sell YES"**
4. USDC returned to your wallet

## Summary

```
1. Choose market → pick YES or NO
2. Enter amount → $25
3. Approve USDC (first time only)
4. Buy → confirm in wallet
5. Wait for result or sell early
```

Congratulations — you're now a prediction market trader! 🚀
