# How to Make Money

## Strategy 1: Buy and Hold Until Resolution

The simplest strategy. Buy shares cheap, wait for the outcome.

```
You think Bitcoin will hit $100K (but market says only 35% chance)

→ Buy YES at $0.35
→ Wait for market to end
→ If BTC hits $100K: you get $1.00 per share → 186% profit
→ If not: you lose $0.35 per share
```

**Best for**: Events you have strong conviction about.

## Strategy 2: Buy Low, Sell High (Trade the Swing)

Don't wait for resolution. Profit from price changes.

```
Monday: YES = $0.30 → Buy 100 shares ($30)
Wednesday: News breaks → YES jumps to $0.55
Wednesday: Sell 100 shares → Get $55
Profit: $25 (83% in 2 days!)
```

**Best for**: Active traders following news closely.

## Strategy 3: Buy NO (Bet Against)

Think something WON'T happen? Buy NO shares.

```
Market: "Will it rain tomorrow?" → YES = $0.85

You check weather: sunny forecast → Buy NO at $0.15
If no rain: NO = $1.00 → Profit $0.85 per share (567%!)
```

**Best for**: Contrarian views, overpriced events.

## Strategy 4: Arbitrage (Risk-Free)

If YES + NO < $1.00, you can profit with zero risk.

```
YES = $0.40, NO = $0.45 → Total = $0.85

Buy 1 YES ($0.40) + 1 NO ($0.45) = $0.85
One of them WILL be worth $1.00
Guaranteed profit: $0.15 per pair
```

## Key Rules

### Do ✅

- Research before trading — knowledge is your edge
- Start with small amounts ($25-$50)
- Diversify across multiple markets
- Set a budget and stick to it
- Sell if your thesis changes

### Don't ❌

- Don't invest more than you can afford to lose
- Don't chase markets near expiry (fees are higher)
- Don't follow the crowd blindly
- Don't ignore the possibility of being wrong
- Don't put all your money in one market

## Understanding Your Risk

```
Max you can lose = what you paid for shares
Max you can gain = $1.00 - what you paid

Bought at $0.20 → Max loss: $0.20, Max gain: $0.80
Bought at $0.80 → Max loss: $0.80, Max gain: $0.20

Lower price = higher potential gain BUT lower probability of winning
```

> ⚠️ **Remember**: Prediction markets involve real money. Never trade with money you need for essentials.
