# Risks & Tips

## Risks You Should Know

### 1. You Can Lose Money
Your shares can become worthless. If you buy YES and the outcome is NO, your shares = $0.

> ⚠️ **Never invest more than you can afford to lose.**

### 2. Markets Can Be Unpredictable
Even "sure things" can go wrong. A 90% probability means 1 in 10 times, the unlikely outcome happens.

### 3. Low Liquidity Risk
Some markets may have low trading volume. This means:
- Harder to buy/sell at the price you want
- Wider spreads (difference between buy and sell price)
- May need to accept worse prices

### 4. Smart Contract Risk
PrediX runs on smart contracts. While audited (264 tests, 22 security fixes), no software is 100% risk-free.

### 5. Oracle Risk
Market resolution depends on oracles (data sources). In rare cases, an oracle could malfunction or provide wrong data.

## Tips for Beginners

### Start Small
```
First week: $25-$50 total
Learn how the platform works
Make mistakes with small amounts
```

### Diversify
```
Don't put all money in 1 market
Spread across 5-10 different markets
Mix: crypto, sports, politics
```

### Do Your Research
```
Don't trade blind
Read the market description carefully
Check the resolution criteria
Understand the oracle source
```

### Set a Budget
```
Decide how much you'll trade this month
Stick to it — win or lose
Never chase losses
```

### Use Limit Orders
```
Market orders execute instantly but may have slippage
Limit orders let you set your exact price
Be patient — good prices come to those who wait
```

### Watch the Fees
```
Trading fees increase near market expiry:
  Normal: 0.5%
  Near expiry: up to 5%

Trade early when fees are low
```

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Why it's bad | What to do instead |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|
| Going all-in on one market | One loss = everything gone | Diversify across markets |
| Buying at $0.95 | Max gain only $0.05, risk losing $0.95 | Look for undervalued markets |
| Ignoring resolution criteria | Market may resolve differently than expected | Read description carefully |
| Trading near expiry | Fees are 5%, spreads are wide | Trade early or use limit orders |
| Never selling early | Missing profit opportunities | Take profits when price moves in your favor |

## Quick Checklist Before Trading

- [ ] Do I understand what this market is about?
- [ ] Do I understand the resolution criteria?
- [ ] Am I comfortable losing this amount?
- [ ] Have I checked the current price and fees?
- [ ] Is there enough liquidity in this market?
